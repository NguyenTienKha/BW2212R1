THE HARLEY-DAVIDSON MUSEUM

Experience our History

Ride along on an epic trip through time at the Harley-Davidson Museum, following our journey from outsider to icon.

Tours

Explore on your own, add an audio tour or check out our Guided Tour options.

GUIDED TOURS

All new guided tours options are available for an add-on to General Admission or by reservation for a group or private tour experience.

AUDIO TOUR

Hear the voices of riders, heroes, and experts from the past and present with our self-guided audio tour. The audio tour can be purchased in the Museum Lobby. In addition to English, the audio tour is available in: Chinese, French, German, Japanese, Portuguese and Spanish.

EXPLORE TOUR

SCOUT MERIT BADGE

Scouts will learn about the history of Harley- Davidson as well as the engineering of motorcycles as they complete all of the requirements to earn an Engineering Merit Badge. The Scout Merit Badge Activity Booklet.

SCOUT VIRTUAL EXPERIENCE

This I-hour online program is offered on Saturdays at 8:30am and Tuesdays at 6:30pm. Scouts will explore the role of an engineer when creating a Harley-Davidson Motorcycle throughout the last 120 years of history.

H.D MUSEUM CAMPUS WALKING TOUR

Available

Download the PDF and follow the path around the 20-acre H-D Museum@ campus for a self-guided tour. Monday - Friday. Tour length is variable as it is self-guided.

Group Visits & Reservation

Is your group ready for a glimpse into a life less ordinary? Visit the Harley-Davidson Museum for a first-class experience that will have your group talking about their museum visit for years to come.

Call (414) 287-2799 or email groups@h-dmuseum.com for more information. Reservations must be made one week in advance.

GROUP ADMISSION RATES (12 OR MORE)

  • Adults - $16
  • Senior - $12
  • Youth - $5
  • K4—High School groups of 12 or more receive the rate of $5 per student with an advance reservation of at least 1 week prior to the visit. Adult chaperones are required: one free per 10 students. Tickets for additional adult chaperones are $16.

GROUP PLANNING DETAILS

  • H.O.G' Chapters (including exclusive H-D museum access)
  • Team-building events, corporate outings, client and customer entertainment
  • Bike groups, book clubs, reunions, church groups, bachelor(ette) parties
  • School field trips, Scout troops, summer camps, daycare and after-school programs
